Scope

This site aggregates publicly available portals and public/open catalogs for discovery and visualization of footprints only. No imagery is hosted or distributed.

We do

  • Show public portal links.
  • Overlay public/open archive footprints on a map.
  • Help reduce discovery time for professionals.

We do NOT

  • No data hosting.
  • No scraping.
  • No vendor inventory APIs for commercial archives.
  • No ordering, pricing, payment, or transactions.
