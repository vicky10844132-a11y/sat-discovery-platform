Scope

SAT-DISCOVERY is a public discovery platform that aggregates links to publicly available satellite data portals and provides tools for searching STAC APIs. We visualize satellite footprints and metadata but do not host, store, or distribute any imagery files.

What We Do

  • Provide links to public satellite data portals and STAC APIs
  • Enable interactive map-based search of satellite imagery metadata
  • Visualize satellite footprints and coverage areas
  • Offer geospatial analysis tools and utilities
  • Help professionals reduce discovery time for satellite data

What We Do NOT

  • No data hosting or caching of imagery files
  • No web scraping or unauthorized data collection
  • No access to vendor inventory APIs for commercial archives
  • No ordering, pricing, payment processing, or transactions
  • No partnerships with commercial data providers
  • No reselling or redistribution of satellite imagery

Data Sources

All satellite data accessed through this platform comes from:

  • Public STAC APIs: AWS EarthSearch, Microsoft Planetary Computer
  • Open Data Programs: Copernicus, Landsat, NASA GIBS
  • Public Portals: Linked external websites maintained by data providers

Users must comply with the terms of service of each individual data provider when accessing or downloading satellite imagery.

Disclaimer

This platform is provided "as is" for informational and research purposes only. We make no guarantees about data availability, accuracy, or completeness. Users are responsible for verifying data quality and compliance with applicable regulations when using satellite imagery for their specific applications.
