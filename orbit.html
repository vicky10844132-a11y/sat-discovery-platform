About Orbit Planning

Orbit planning helps you understand when satellites will pass over your area of interest. This information is useful for:

  • Planning data acquisition campaigns
  • Understanding satellite revisit patterns
  • Identifying optimal observation windows
  • Coordinating with satellite operators for tasking

Note: Actual satellite availability and data acquisition depend on various factors including weather, satellite health, and operational priorities.
